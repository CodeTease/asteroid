# Bestiary

A guide to the hostile entities found in the Asteroid Destroyer universe.

## Standard Enemies (Normal Mode)

These enemies appear during the first 300 seconds of gameplay.

*   **Asteroid:** The basic hazard. comes in various sizes. Splits into smaller pieces when destroyed.
*   **Scout:** Fast, lightly armored units that attempt to crash into the player.
*   **Brute:** High HP, slow-moving ships that act as damage sponges.
*   **Shooter:** Stationary or slow-moving units that fire projectiles at the player.

## Void Legion (Void Mode)

Unleashed after the Initial Final Boss falls. These enemies are visually distinct and possess dangerous abilities.

*   **Orbiter:** Circles the player, making them hard to hit with forward-firing weapons.
*   **Weaver:** Moves in unpredictable patterns, laying traps or weaving between projectiles.
*   **Bulwark:** Heavily armored units that shield other enemies behind them.
*   **Teleporter:** Instantly changes position to ambush the player.
*   **Juggler:** Bounces projectiles back or manipulates physics.
*   **Sizzler:** Applies Heat damage to the player upon proximity, risking Overheat.
*   **Breacher (Crisis):** extremely fast units designed specifically to damage the Void Barrier.
*   **Brick Wall (Crisis):** Massive, passive structure with immense HP that blocks movement and projectiles.
*   **Ghost Asteroid (Extended):** Invisible hazards revealed only by close proximity to the player or allies.

## Bosses

Major encounters that define the transition between game phases.

### 1. Initial Final Boss
*   **Encounter:** 300s (Global Time).
*   **Role:** The Gatekeeper to the Void.
*   **Mechanics:** Uses standard projectile patterns but with high density.
*   **Reward:** Unlocks Void Mode and Void Skills.

### 2. Behemoth
*   **Encounter:** 150s (Void Time).
*   **Role:** The Guardian of Extended Mode.
*   **Type:** Turret / Mini-Boss.
*   **Mechanics:**
    *   Stationary but rotates to track the player.
    *   70% Resistance to Laser Ally.
    *   Immune to AI Ally projectiles.
    *   Fires **Behemoth Bombs** (large AoE).
*   **Reward:** Unlocks Void Mode: Extended and the **Vamp Ally**.

### 3. Monolith
*   **Encounter:** 300s (Void Time).
*   **Role:** The Sentinel of Crisis Mode.
*   **Type:** Massive Structure.
*   **Mechanics:**
    *   High defensive resistances.
    *   **Cooling Nodes:** Weak points on the structure. Destroying them damages the boss, stuns it, and clears player Heat.
*   **Reward:** Unlocks Void Mode: Crisis ("Five Minutes of Hell").

### 4. Afterimage
*   **Encounter:** 600s (Void Time).
*   **Role:** The Final Challenge.
*   **Mechanics:**
    *   Extremely fast movement.
    *   "Glitchy" appearance and hitboxes.
    *   Utilizes advanced movement akin to the Crisis Mode "Drift" and "Inversion" mechanics.
